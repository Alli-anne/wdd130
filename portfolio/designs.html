Little Woman

With this project I was asked to make a poster for a production.
In this poster it has photos of all the cast members. I gave it a college type
of look to give it an older look. I loved this project and being able to add so much
personality of this group and feel like that they are really apart of the show. Along with
this I also designed the tickets and playbill with taking all the production photos.

Decoding Dyslexia Utah

I had the chance to be able to work with Decoding Dyslexia Utah
to do some work for their conference. This includes making post cards and trifolds to send to potential attenders
of the confernce. I also got to make a poster for all the sponsors. Along with this I was able to a schedule with the different rooms speakers.
I was apart of this group a couple of years before making this logo. This group talks to teachers about dyslexia to help
others be able to understand it better. As being apart of this group I asked if there was anything they needed help with. They were looking
for a new logo and asked me to make a couple of designs that could be used as the logo. This was very important to me because this group does so much great
work for people and being able to help even after I left the group meant so much to me.
